About Me

Hi, I’m Cameron, an aspiring developer looking to make my mark on the world of Web and UX!

 I am a student at Weber State University majoring in Web and User Experience. I will be graduating with my Associate degree in December and will then set my sights on working towards my bachelors degree. Currently, I am on track to graduate with my bachelors in December 2026. I have completed each of my major related courses with an A grade and I am maintaining an overall 3.91 GPA. I have been able to keep up these grades while working 32+ hours a week and being a full-time student. I am a very hard-working individual who gets things done in both work and school.

When I have some spare time I really enjoy gaming with my friends. If I’m feeling a little more adventurous on a given day then I also really enjoy hiking and bike riding. I also love going to my local arcades as I am weirdly good at the game Crossy Road and through being good at this game I have won 3 Nintendo Switches, 3 Xboxes, a PS4 Pro, 2 pairs of Airpods, a scooter, and more! 

When I graduate with my bachelors, I intend to find employment as a full-stack web developer and as such I will be starting full-stack related courses during this next semester. Until that point, I am looking for internship opportunities that would allow me to get some experience in the field of Web and UX.
