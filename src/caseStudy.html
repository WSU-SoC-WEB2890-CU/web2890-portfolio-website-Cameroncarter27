Project Overview

For this project my main role was being the sole coder of the site as most of the general design work had been planned out before I undertook the project. My goal with this project was to make a visually pleasing and professional site that laid out all necessary information in an intuitive manner. In the end, I believe I achieved this goal as I produced what I see as an effective, professional looking website that should be rather simple for users to navigate.

When it came time for me to pick a color palette for this website I knew I wanted something simple but effective and professional. With these guidelines in mind I immediately fell in love with the color scheme shown above. I thought that these colors would work well together as the grays help make the site look professional and classy but then you add the blues and it gives it a nice little splash of color that just feels right. I felt like this classiness would fit well with the nature of this site and would just overall complement it.
