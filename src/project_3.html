Modern Arcade Guides (Design #2)

Project Details

This site was targeted towards older teenagers who enjoy going to the arcade and gaming. As this was an alternative version of one of my other sites this site took me significantly less time to complete and ended up finishing it after about 10 hours of work.

Purpose and Scope

I made this redesign with the same purpose of having a space to put my arcade gaming knowledge into that could be shared with others. This site had the same five pages as the previous design as well.

Design Decisions

I wanted this version of the site to look a little more modern and not as focused on a younger audience. This is why I went with the darker colors and the arcade picture in the header. I feel like this look would be much more appealing to older teenagers looking for arcade guides.

Required Elements

  • HTML element aided by Javascript
  • Header background image
  • General use of Bootstrap throughout the design

Results

By the end of this project I ended up with a more sleek look to a design I had previously done. Through the use of Bootstrap and Javascript I was able to make a very professional site that I believe would appeal nicely to older teenagers. This project earned me an A grade.
